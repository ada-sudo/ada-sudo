Hello, I'm Beatrice Varriale, born in 1998. This portfolio is a visual diary, capturing life's moments through collage art. Inspired by the cultural richness of Naples, my hometown, each piece reflects the vibrant chaos of existence.

Additionally, a significant part of my work is dedicated to advocating for freedom in Palestine, turning each collage into a visual narrative for justice and liberation. Join me on this journey through life, culture, and a commitment to meaningful causes.
